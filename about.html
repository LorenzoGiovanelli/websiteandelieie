Company Overview

The ANDELEIE FOUNDATION LIMITED was born in 2017, from the will and commitment of a wealthy Chinese entrepreneur, who mainly markets petroleum products in Chinese and European territory and is expanding throughout the world. It operates and grows in the European cradle and establishes itself in the years in Europe and Asia.

The company structure is enriched with new skills with contacts in new areas, expanding its operating margins also to the African continent, North America, and throughout the Middle East, intensifying trade exchanges. The expansion of commercial borders contributes to the completion of the range of products that ANDELEIE FOUNDATION LIMITED is today able to offer, from petroleum products to the range of industrial metals.

Trading brings together different entrepreneurial realities which are sometimes complementary. ANDELEIE FOUNDATION LIMITED knows the value of synergies and aims to create a network of closely interconnected partners, capable of operating as a single group.

Today it moves easily both in terms of petroleum products, in the complete range, and in coal, and in metals for industry, coming from all over the world.

The geographical positioning of the company and the partners who make up the staff guarantees extreme agility both for the proximity to the areas of interest and for the completeness of the financial and logistical services offered.

Our daily commitment is to guarantee customer satisfaction, selecting for them the best of what our suppliers and producers can offer us, optimizing and improving the service from procurement to delivery and completion of paperwork. We dedicate no less effort to researching new products for increasingly demanding customers, but on the other hand this is the spirit that moves us and unites us.

We are currently working to satisfy the demand of both the European market and the Asian market, with the introduction of new origins.

Our vision of the future is full of feelings that prevail over the simple search for profit, we are in fact engaged in projects in which at the center of the business is the search for improving the living conditions of the weakest links in the commercial chain, i.e. the areas disadvantaged people in the world, often forgotten by the logic of business.

Respect for the environment sees us committed to the forefront in the field of processing and production, encouraging and supporting the creation of sustainable products for the planet. We think there can be no profit without a future

Our company always adheres to people-oriented and honest business philosophy management, innovation and development. With flexible management mechanism and strict quality management, we strictly control all aspects of procurement, raw materials, quality control, etc. To provide customers with the best quality products and best service.
